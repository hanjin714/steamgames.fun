Author and Editor

steamgames.fun is edited as an independent personal guide site for Steam players who want practical first-session advice, system explanations, and source-aware notes for new or actively updated games.

The site is not affiliated with Valve, Steam, developers, or publishers. Game names and store assets are used for identification, commentary, and guide context.

Guide style

The writing focuses on actions a player can take: what to do first, what to delay, what mistakes cost the most time, and how to understand a system before copying a build. The site favors beginner and returning-player clarity over exhaustive wiki-style data dumps.

For corrections or suggestions, email contact@steamgames.fun with the page URL, the game version or date, and the specific section that should be reviewed.

Disclosure

steamgames.fun may use Google AdSense. Ads are separated from guide choices, and the site does not publish paid placement or ask readers to interact with ads.
